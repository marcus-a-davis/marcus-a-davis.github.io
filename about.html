About me

Marcus A. Davis is from Chicago where he studied audio engineering at Columbia College Chicago and worked as a freelancer before taking up programming in 2014.

He's an effective altruist and board member of Charity Science, which raises money for effective charities.

Marcus is also a recovering sports fan and, via the rigorous examination of a Facebook post, recently discovered he's the only person who is a fan of synth pop, lyrics-driven rap, and utilitarian ethics.
